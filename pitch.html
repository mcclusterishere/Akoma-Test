How you sell books you don't stock

  • The search bar on your own site (live now) — millions of titles, covers included, free API, every order routed to your inbox. The customer never leaves visitakoma.com.
  • Ingram iPage — the wholesale account every indie runs on: apply free with your EIN + resale certificate, order at trade discount, books land in 1–3 days for pickup-with-a-coffee.
  • Bookshop.org bookstore affiliate — 30% of cover price on online sales they fulfill, zero inventory (requires ABA membership; 10% tier without it).
  • Libro.fm partnership — audiobooks under your brand, revenue split, nothing to stock.
  • Rare & vintage — the site's rare filter finds it; we source from AbeBooks/Biblio dealer networks and quote with 30–50% margin.
